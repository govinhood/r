About Station

Govinhood FM is Nepal’s premier radio destination, streaming non-stop music 24 hours a day to listeners around the world. Our carefully curated playlists blend soulful Nepali Folk, nostalgic 90’s Pop classics, and the latest modern Nepali hits—seamlessly uniting tradition with contemporary sound.
More than just a radio station, Govinhood FM is a cultural bridge that connects generations and celebrates the spirit, stories, and emotions of Nepal. Wherever you are, we bring the heartbeat of Nepal straight to your ears—anytime, anywhere.
